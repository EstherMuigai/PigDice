RULES


  • 1.A player will get the chance to roll the dice
  • 2.The player can view their points each time they roll the dice at the bottom of their console
  • 3.Each player's score is displayed at the center of their console
  • 4.If a player rolls a 1, their score is cleared and the chance goes to the other player
  • 5.If a player holds, their score is held and the other player gets to play
  • The first player to reach a score of 100 is the winner
